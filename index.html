About

Hello, my name is Ma(r)ek, I'm from Slovakia and I play only premium games on my iPhone and iPad. After finishing a few I started my private list. After finishing more 300 I decided to share my list online. By finishing I mean that I either saw the "thanks for playing" screen or the credits roll. By premium I mean games for which I pay upfornt money and I get the whole experience. I'm also OK with trying games for free and then paying to unlock content. I consider games on subscription services like Apple Arcade or Netflix premium, too. But, I avoid games containing purchases for ads removal or games with double currencies. There are some exceptions in my list, but those all have their own reasons.
