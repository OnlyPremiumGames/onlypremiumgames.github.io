About

Hello, my name is Ma(r)ek, I'm from Slovakia and I play only premium games on my iPhone and iPad. After finishing more than 300 of them I realized that someone might find it helpful to read some quick thoughts on these games.

By finishing I mean that I either saw the "thanks for playing" screen or the credits roll.

By premium I mean games for which I pay upfornt money and I get the whole experience. I'm also OK with trying games for free and then paying to unlock content. I consider games on subscription services like Apple Arcade or Netflix premium, too.

But, I avoid games containing purchases for ads removal or games with double currencies.

There are some exceptions in my list, but those all have their own reasons.

Rules

If I'll ask you for a promo code please keep the following in mind:

  • I only post my thoughts on games I finish.
  • I rarely don't finish games I start playing and enjoy.
  • I play games new and old - in no particular order.

    • If your game is not on my site that either means I did not enjoy it (sorry!) or I did not finish it, yet.
